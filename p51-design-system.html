01 / Manifesto

A system,
not a style.

The decisions encoded here outlast their authors. Open this file in five years; the logic still resolves.

We don't decorate information. We give it structural integrity — and let it speak.

Project 51 is the synthesis of two complete disciplines: visual craft and system engineering. The work we ship looks designed. It also runs. Both halves are non-negotiable.

This document codifies the geometry, motion, and language of that synthesis. It is both a constraint and an unfair advantage. Every component descends from the same primitive — the intersection of two squares — so that an interface designed today can be extended ten years from now without contradiction.

Read this once. Internalize it. Then design with absolute confidence.

P · 01

Silent Authority

Volume is for the inexperienced. Confidence is the absence of decoration.

P · 02

Geometric Honesty

Every dimension traces back to an 8-pixel base unit or the 0.625 intersection ratio. No arbitrary numbers.

P · 03

Color as Data

Color carries information. It does not carry mood. If a color does not name a data type, it does not appear.

P · 04

Motion as Logic

Things move because they're aligning, revealing, or making space. Never to entertain.

P · 05

Reduction by Default

If an element can be removed without loss of meaning, it must be removed. Always.

P · 06

The Hairline Standard

Borders are 1 pixel. Shadows are forbidden. Depth comes from light surfaces and structural lines, not from blur.

02 / Mark

Two entities,
one intersection.

Two equal squares, offset along the diagonal by 30 units. Where they cross, a 50-unit core forms. That core is the brand.

Construction · Geometry

Each square is 80 units. Square B is offset (+30, +30) from Square A. Their intersection — a 50×50 core — is filled solid. The ratio 50/80 = 0.625 is the foundational proportion of the entire visual system.

14 / Rules

What we do.
What we never do.

The mark is precise. Misuse erodes meaning. Treat these rules as load-bearing.

Do · Use as designed

Default proportions, ratios, and stroke weights. The mark is engineered; do not retune it.

Do · Allow clear space

One full square unit of breathing room around the mark — always.

Do · Mono only

Pure black or pure off-white only. Inverted variant on light surfaces. Nothing in between.

Don't · Distort proportions

Never stretch, squash, or skew the mark. Squares are squares.

Don't · Recolor

No colored variants. No gradients. The mark is monochrome by law.

Don't · Add effects

No drop shadows, glows, bevels, or 3D treatments. The mark is flat geometry.
