04 / GEOMETRY
geometry · the mark

The mark is not metaphor.
The mark is the method, drawn.

Two equal squares, offset along the diagonal by 30 units. Where they cross, a 50-unit core forms. The ratio 50/80 — 0.625 — propagates outward as the dominant proportion of the entire system. Every column. Every reading measure. Every dialog width.
